Two-Layer Tic-Tac-Toe

Make a selection to start playing.







How to Play

1. Select "X" or "O" to start the game.
2. "X" always goes first.
3. You can only choose spaces that are empty or occupied as single layer.
4. You cannot choose boxes chosen in the opponent's previous turn.
5. Lock a box by clicking on the box twice in two turns.
6. Win with horizontal, vertical, or diagonal 3-in-a-row.
7. You can win with single layers or together with locked boxes.
